About us

A global team building
real websites.

VividLaunchForge is a collaborative team of BYU-Pathway students from across Africa, gaining real-world skills while helping others bring their ideas to life.

Our story

VividLaunchForge began as a simple mentoring session — just a few young people looking for guidance. With the leadership of our Pathway career coach, that small gathering grew into a powerful international program where students learn AI skills, build real websites, strengthen communication, and develop career confidence.

Every Tuesday evening, our team meets for a focused online session — teaching, guiding, and empowering each other to build the future. We work across four countries, managing time zones, internet challenges, and shared responsibilities to deliver professional results.

We believe that skills open doors, small daily efforts create big long-term results, and self-reliance empowers you to lift others. These principles guide every session and every project.
